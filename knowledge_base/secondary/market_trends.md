# Market Trends — Café Industry 2024–2025

## Market Size
- Global coffee shop market: $237 billion (2024), projected $310 billion by 2029
- Independent cafés growing 5.8% annually — faster than chains (3.2%)
- Young consumers 18–30 account for 62% of café visits globally

## Trend 1: The Third Place Revival
- 71% of Gen Z report feeling lonely regularly (Cigna, 2024)
- 64% of young people actively seek physical spaces outside home and work
- Cafés are the number 1 third place chosen by 18–30 year olds
- "Stay as long as you want" cafés see 2.4x higher average spend per visit

## Trend 2: Identity-Driven Consumption
- 73% of Gen Z choose brands that reflect their personal identity (McKinsey, 2024)
- Gen Z is 2.5x more likely than millennials to pay premium for identity-aligned brands
- 58% of Gen Z shared a café on social media because "it looked good" — not the food
- Spaces that make customers feel "smart" or "cultured" have 41% higher return rates

## Trend 3: Starbucks Losing Gen Z
- Starbucks same-store sales declined in Q3 2024
- Among 18–24 year olds, Starbucks brand perception dropped 12 points (YouGov, 2024)
- StarbucksIsBasic has 180 million TikTok views
- 44% of former Starbucks customers aged 18–28 switched for "better vibe"
- Independent cafés with strong local identity absorbed 60% of lost young customers

## Trend 4: Study Café Model
- Study café concept grew 340% in Asia between 2020–2024
- Average dwell time: 3.2 hours vs 25 minutes in standard cafés
- Average spend per visit: 2.8x higher than standard cafés
- 82% of university students prefer studying in cafés over university libraries

## Trend 5: Social Media Discovery
- 67% of Gen Z discovers new cafés through TikTok (Toast, 2024)
- "Study café aesthetic" TikTok content has 4.2 billion views
- Cafés with strong visual identity generate 4.7x more user-generated content
- First 30 days of social presence sets algorithm trajectory for months

## Trend 6: Micro-Influencer Marketing
- Micro-influencers have 60% higher engagement than mega-influencers
- Local micro-influencers cost $50–$300 per post
- One micro-influencer post drives average 180 new visitors in first week
