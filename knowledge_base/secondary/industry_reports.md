# Industry Reports — Café Sector 2024–2025

## Report 1: NCA State of Coffee 2024
- Coffee consumption among 18–24 grew 14% in 2024 — highest growth demographic
- 41% of students use cafés as primary study location
- Top reason 18–30 choose a café: atmosphere (44%), convenience (31%), price (25%)
- Specialty independent cafés growing 3x faster than chains in urban markets

IMPLICATION: Atmosphere is the #1 purchase driver. Every design decision is a marketing decision.

## Report 2: Deloitte Gen Z Consumer Behavior 2024
- Gen Z spends 23% more per café visit than Millennials
- 78% of Gen Z consider brand aesthetic and values BEFORE their first visit
- Social proof (friends going, influencers posting) is the primary trigger for first visits
- Gen Z most likely to become brand ambassadors — they post, recommend, and defend brands they love
- 61% prefer local businesses over chains when quality is comparable

IMPLICATION: Brand identity must be strong BEFORE opening. Gen Z decides before they visit.

## Report 3: Cigna Loneliness Economy 2024
- 71% of Gen Z feel lonely regularly — highest of any generation
- Physical third places are the most effective solution to social isolation
- Young people with a "regular spot" report 34% higher wellbeing scores
- Brands that create community have 3–5x higher customer lifetime value

IMPLICATION: My Space addresses a genuine social need — not just a coffee preference.

## Report 4: Meta Social Media & Food Discovery 2024
- Café content on TikTok generates 18.4 billion monthly views
- "Study café aesthetic" content has 4.2 billion views — no dominant brand owns it yet
- User-generated content drives 5x higher conversion than brand-posted content
- Most shared café content: window seats with natural light (42%)
- Peak engagement: Sunday 7–9pm and weekday mornings 8–10am

IMPLICATION: Design the space for content creation. My Space can own the study café niche on TikTok.

## Report 5: Specialty Coffee Association Economics 2024
- Average spend in study café per visit: €14–18 (vs €6–9 standard café)
- Regular conversion rate: 68% of first-time visitors return within 2 weeks
- Monthly membership model: €25/month × 80 members = €2,000 passive monthly revenue
- Cafés with strong brand identity reach profitability 40% faster than unbranded concepts

IMPLICATION: The study café model is better economics AND better community building.
