# My Space Café — Concept & Services

## The Concept
My Space Café is a café-library hybrid where the environment is the product.
Coffee is excellent — but it is the vehicle, not the destination.
The destination is the feeling of being in a space designed for people like you.

## Format
- Size: 80–120 seats across two zones
- Hours: 8am–10pm daily
- Offerings: Specialty coffee, teas, cold drinks, light food
- Library: 300–500 curated books available to read in-café
- WiFi: Fast, free, always — non-negotiable
- Power outlets: At every seat

## Revenue Streams
1. Food & beverage (primary)
2. Private evening event hire
3. Monthly membership card (unlimited filter coffee)
4. Merchandise (branded tote bags, notebooks, mugs)

## Consultant Services

### Service 1: Brand Identity Package
- Brand name, tagline, positioning statement

- Voice, tone, messaging guidelines
- Visual direction brief
- Competitor differentiation strategy
- Complete brand bible document

### Service 2: Grand Opening Campaign
- Phase 1: Pre-launch (4 weeks of content + tactics)
- Phase 2: Opening week (day-by-day calendar, event strategy)
- Phase 3: First 30 days retention plan
- 15 ready-to-post captions
- 3 TikTok video concepts
- Opening day experience design

## Key Insight
Most cafés fail not because of bad coffee — but because of no clear identity.
We build the brand BEFORE the furniture is ordered.
When the doors open, there is already a community waiting.
