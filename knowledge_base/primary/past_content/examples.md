# My Space Café — Content Examples

## PRE-LAUNCH

### Post 1 — Mystery Teaser (6 weeks before)
"Something's coming to [City].
A space that actually feels like yours.
Follow to be first."
WHY IT WORKS: Creates intrigue. "Actually feels like yours" speaks to the pain point.

### Post 2 — Space Reveal (3 weeks before)
"Window seat. Power outlet. Perfect coffee.
And absolutely no one rushing you.
Opening [Month]. Save your spot."
WHY IT WORKS: Lists exactly what students want. Creates urgency without discounts.

### Post 3 — Community Call (2 weeks before)
"We're looking for the people who already know they belong here.
You know who you are.
Opening [Date]. This is My Space — and it's yours too."
WHY IT WORKS: Makes the reader self-select. They feel seen before visiting.

## OPENING WEEK

### Post 4 — Opening Day
"Today is day one.
The espresso machine is running. The books are on the shelves. Your seat is waiting.
My Space is open. Come in."
WHY IT WORKS: Short, present tense, personal invitation.

### TikTok Script — Opening Tour
HOOK: "We didn't open another coffee shop."
VIDEO: Quick cuts — bookshelves, window seats, espresso machine, student with laptop
VOICEOVER: "We opened the place you have been looking for. Where you can stay four hours and no one gives you a look. Where the vibe is actually yours."
CLOSING: "Find your spot. [Address]"

## CONTENT RULES
1. Lead with a feeling — never a product feature
2. "You" language in every post
3. Max 2 sentences per paragraph
4. Real visuals only — no stock photos
5. End with belonging — every post should make reader think "that is my kind of place"
