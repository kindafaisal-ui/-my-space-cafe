# My Space Café — Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity
**Brand Name:** My Space Café
**Tagline:** "This is My Space. Make it yours."
**Concept:** A modern café-library hybrid for young people aged 18–30. Not just a place to get coffee — a place to belong.

## The Problem We Solve
Most cafés treat young people as customers to turn over quickly.
Starbucks is efficient but soulless. Libraries are quiet but cold. Home is comfortable but isolating.
Young people have nowhere that is truly THEIRS. My Space fills that gap.

## Brand Mission
To create a physical space where young people feel genuinely at home — intellectually stimulated, socially comfortable, and proud to be seen there.

## Brand Values
1. Belonging — Everyone who walks in should feel expected, not tolerated
2. Authenticity — Real vibes, real people, no performance
3. Ambition — We celebrate people who are going somewhere
4. Community over transaction — We measure success in regulars, not receipts

## Brand Voice
- Tone: Warm, bold, confident — like a cool friend who believes in you
- Short sentences. Present tense. Second person ("you", "your").
- NEVER say: "We are passionate about coffee", "Premium quality", "Best-in-class"
- ALWAYS: Make the reader the main character. Specific, visual, emotional.

## Target Audience
Primary: Students aged 18–24 — study in cafés 3–6 hours/week, share aesthetic spaces on social media
Secondary: Young professionals 24–30 — work remotely, value design and atmosphere

## What My Space Really Sells
1. Identity — being seen at My Space says something good about you
2. Productivity — the environment makes you feel capable
3. Community — regulars become familiar faces, then friends
4. Pride — you will post about it because it looks as good as it feels

## Space Design
- Library zone: quiet, focused, warm lighting, bookshelves
- Social zone: collaborative tables, good for group work
- Window seats: the ones people post on Instagram
- Music: curated lo-fi and jazz playlists — never random
